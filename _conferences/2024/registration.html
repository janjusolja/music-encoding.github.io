Conference Registration

Registration for the Music Encoding Conference is now open. Early registration ends on April 7, and regular registration ends on May 3.

Early registration for in-person attendance (prior to April 7): $120.00

Regular registration for in-person attendance (after April 7): $150.00

Registration for online-only attendance: $25.00

Student registration for in-person attendance: $50.00

Please follow this link https://bit.ly/MEC2024Register to register. You may also book your ticket for Wednesday evening's conference dinner via the registration link.

Contact Maristella.Feustle@unt.edu for assistance.
